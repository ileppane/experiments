Binary Choices and Your Payment:

In the following part, you are asked to make investment decisions based on binary choices. Same with the Part 2, you are assigned with {{endowment}} (= {{pound}}). In total, 324 trials will be presented to you. As is shown in the Figure 2 below, in each trial, you will see the a lottery and a monetary amount, which is the price tag of that lottery. You can purchase the lottery by choosing it, or give up the purchasing opportunity by choosing the price tag.

After completing this section, one random trial will be selected to count. If you chose the lottery in that trial, you will receive it at its selling price. On the other hand, if the price tag was chosen, you will keep the entire {{endowment}} and leave without a lottery.

Further Instructions:

To make the choice between the lottery and the price tag, you can and can only use the left key (←) and the right key (→) on your keyboard, they are located on the bottom-right of a standard keyboard. To note, the side of display of these two items on screen will vary, in each trial you need to observe the their location and use the corresponsing key to make selection.

When the choice trials start, we would like to ask you to maximise your browser and concentrate on making investment choices without interruptions. You will have opportunities to take two breaks after the trial 108 and 216.

Before starting the actual trials, we prepared a short practice session for you to be familiar with the mechanism in this section. This practice session only has six trials, including a rest point in the middle. These trials will NOT count for payment.
